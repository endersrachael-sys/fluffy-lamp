# JarDIYn 10/10 Product Roadmap

## P0 — Live app cleanup

- Hide Developer Panel by default.
- Make homepage homeowner-first.
- Fix raw markdown.
- Make Garden Passport clear and saved.
- Add clear response trace.
- Keep roadmap features labeled as future.

## P1 — Brass MVP

- Garden Passport.
- Demo Scenario Launcher.
- Plan Check Mode.
- Generate Garden Plan.
- Source/tool trace.
- Evaluation tests.

## P2 — Trust and retention

- Garden Recovery Mode.
- Saved local history timeline.
- Better plant cache.
- Local pilot workflow.
- Exportable PDF reports.

## P3 — Accounts

- Sign in with Google / Apple / email.
- Cloud-saved properties.
- Shared household/professional access.
- Permission model.

## P4 — Property Intelligence

- Property Passport.
- Municipal GIS upload/import.
- Public parcel layers.
- Soil, slope, canopy, impervious surface context.
- Paid satellite/drone partner reports.

## P5 — Ecosystem

- Nursery shopping list handoff.
- Landscaper proposal builder.
- Arborist/irrigation review requests.
- Local “The Crew” community.
- Marketplace only after trust is earned.
